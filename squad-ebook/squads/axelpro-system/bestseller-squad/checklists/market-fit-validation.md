# Market Fit Validation Checklist

```yaml
checklist:
  id: market-fit-validation
  version: 1.0.0
  created: 2026-03-11
  purpose: "Ensure the book's core promise and positioning are aligned with real market demand."
  mode: blocking
```

---

## Reader Pain Point Alignment

- [ ] **Urgency:** Is the problem being solved urgent for the reader?
- [ ] **Specificity:** Is the pain point specific rather than a broad theme?
- [ ] **Language Match:** Does the promise use the same terms readers use in their reviews/complaints?

## Competitive Positioning

- [ ] **Market Gap:** Does the book address a specific unmet need identified in competitor reviews?
- [ ] **Unique Mechanism:** Is the proposed method or approach clearly different from existing top-sellers?
- [ ] **Micro-Market Focus:** Is the initial target audience small enough to dominate but large enough to scale?

## Promise Strength (Blocking)

- [ ] **Instant Clarity:** Can a stranger understand the value proposition in 10 seconds?
- [ ] **Measurable Outcome:** Is the result promised concrete and perceivable?
- [ ] **Risk Reduction:** Does the promise explicitly lower the barrier to entry (e.g., "even without experience")?
