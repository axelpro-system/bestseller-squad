# Chapter Quality Gate Checklist

```yaml
checklist:
  id: chapter-quality-gate
  version: 1.0.0
  created: 2026-03-11
  purpose: "Validate that an individual chapter meets bestseller engagement and application standards."
  mode: blocking
```

---

## Content & Structure

- [ ] **Pattern Interrupt:** Does the chapter start with a compelling hook or counter-intuitive statement?
- [ ] **Emotional Identification:** Does the content make the reader feel understood ("This is me")?
- [ ] **Practical Clarity:** Is the method or step explained without unnecessary jargon or complexity?
- [ ] **Immediate Application:** Does the chapter answer "What do I do now?" with a concrete action?

## Engagement & Value

- [ ] **Micro-Victory:** Is there a small win the reader can achieve immediately?
- [ ] **Logical Flow:** Does this chapter naturally follow the previous one and prepare for the next?
- [ ] **Language Efficiency:** Is the writing concise and focused on results rather than theory?
- [ ] **Continuity Hook:** Does the end of the chapter create anticipation for the next part?

## Veto Conditions (Blocking)

- [ ] Chapter is purely theoretical with no practical application.
- [ ] Chapter attempts to solve more than one major problem.
- [ ] Language is overly academic or difficult to follow.
- [ ] No clear next step for the reader.
