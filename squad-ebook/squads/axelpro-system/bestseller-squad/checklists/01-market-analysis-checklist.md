# Market Analysis Completion Checklist

[[LLM: VALIDATION - MARKET ANALYSIS PHASE

This checklist validates that all market analysis tasks have been completed with sufficient quality before proceeding to the Promise phase.

PHASE GOALS:
- Understand the market landscape
- Identify reader problems deeply
- Know the competition
- Find gaps to exploit

VALIDATION PRINCIPLES:
1. Completeness - All required data gathered
2. Quality - Data is current and reliable
3. Actionability - Insights lead to clear next steps
4. Documentation - Findings are recorded clearly]]

## 1. MARKET TRENDS ANALYSIS

[[LLM: Verify that market trends analysis includes:

1. Data sources identified and documented
2. At least 3 trending topics analyzed
3. Cycle phase identified for each trend
4. Quantitative data included (search volume, sales, rankings)
5. Seasonal patterns documented
6. Recommendations provided]]

- [ ] Data sources documented (Amazon, Google Trends, Goodreads, etc.)
- [ ] At least 3 trending topics identified and analyzed
- [ ] Each trend has cycle phase (emerging/growing/peak/declining)
- [ ] Quantitative data included (numbers, percentages, rankings)
- [ ] Seasonal patterns identified
- [ ] Recommendations with Go/No-Go/Wait for each trend
- [ ] Executive summary provided

## 2. READER PAINS IDENTIFICATION

[[LLM: Verify that reader pain points include:

1. Target audience clearly defined
2. At least 10 specific pain points identified
3. Pain intensity ranked (1-10)
4. Reader language captured (exact quotes)
5. Emotional drivers identified
6. Primary pain prioritized]]

- [ ] Target audience demographics documented
- [ ] At least 10 specific pain points identified
- [ ] Pain points categorized by intensity (1-10)
- [ ] Reader language captured (direct quotes)
- [ ] Emotional drivers identified (fear, frustration, hope)
- [ ] Primary pain clearly prioritized
- [ ] Pain points validated with sources

## 3. COMPETITOR EVALUATION

[[LLM: Verify that competitive analysis includes:

1. At least 10 competitor titles analyzed
2. Top 3 competitors detailed (SWOT)
3. Common weaknesses in reviews documented
4. Competitor positioning mapped
5. Gap opportunities identified
6. Pricing analysis included]]

- [ ] At least 10 competitor titles documented
- [ ] Top 3 competitors have detailed SWOT analysis
- [ ] Common weaknesses from reviews identified
- [ ] Competitor promises mapped
- [ ] Gap opportunities documented
- [ ] Pricing range analyzed

## 4. MARKET GAPS IDENTIFIED

[[LLM: Verify that market gap analysis includes:

1. Data from all 3 previous analyses cross-referenced
2. At least 5 gap opportunities identified
3. Each gap validated with evidence
4. Priority scoring applied (7+ minimum)
5. Target audience specific for each gap
6. Unique angle defined]]

- [ ] Cross-reference of trends, pains, and competition complete
- [ ] At least 5 gap opportunities identified
- [ ] Each gap has validation evidence
- [ ] Priority scoring applied (gaps ranked)
- [ ] Target audience specific for each gap
- [ ] Unique angle defined for top gaps
- [ ] Final recommendation provided
