```yaml
  id: bestseller-process

  mode: recommended
```

---

## Step 1: Market Observation

- [ ] Identified the top-ranked books in the target category.
- [ ] Analyzed 1, 2, and 3-star reviews for recurring complaints and unmet needs.
- [ ] Mapped the specific language and emotional terms used by readers.
- [ ] Identified at least 3 patterns of reader behavior/desires.

## Step 2: Problem-to-Promise Transformation

- [ ] Selected a single, dominant "pain point" to resolve.
- [ ] Drafted a promise that is specific, direct, and outcome-oriented.
- [ ] Verified that the promise reduces effort, time, or risk.
- [ ] Tested the promise for immediate clarity (understood in < 10 seconds).

## Step 3: Book Structuring

- [ ] Defined a logical flow that leads to an inevitable transformation.
- [ ] Included a "Break of Illusion" phase at the beginning.
- [ ] Ensured each chapter builds upon the previous one.
- [ ] Verified that every chapter directly serves the main promise.

## Step 4: Engaging Chapter Creation

- [ ] Each chapter starts with a "Pattern Interrupt" to grab attention.
- [ ] Included practical exercises or actionable steps in every chapter.
- [ ] Designed at least one "Micro-Victory" per chapter for the reader.
- [ ] Used simple and efficient language (no unnecessary jargon).

## Step 5: Building Continuity

- [ ] Implemented continuity hooks at the end of each chapter.
- [ ] Ensured "Partial Promise Fulfillment" to keep the reader curious.
- [ ] Verified structural dependency between chapters.
- [ ] Maintained a rhythm that balances depth and readability.

## Step 6: Authority & Social Proof

- [ ] Integrated the author's expertise through practical application, not just theory.
- [ ] Included case studies, examples, or real-life scenarios.
- [ ] Placed calls to action for reviews at moments of perceived value.
- [ ] Established authority through clarity and coherence of the method.

## Step 7: Book as an Asset

- [ ] Defined the long-term strategic value of the book for the author's business.
- [ ] Identified specific micro-markets for initial domination.
- [ ] Created a plan for scaling impact through adjacent markets.
- [ ] Verified the book "explains itself" without external intervention.
