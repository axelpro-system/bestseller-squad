# Engagement & Authority Completion Checklist

[[LLM: VALIDATION - ENGAGEMENT & AUTHORITY PHASE

This checklist validates that reader engagement strategies and author authority elements have been properly implemented.

PHASE GOALS:
- Design complete reader journey
- Implement continuity hooks
- Minimize drop-off points
- Establish author credibility
- Generate social proof elements
- Encourage reader reviews

VALIDATION PRINCIPLES:
1. Reader Experience - Journey designed end-to-end
2. Momentum - Hooks keep reading
3. Retention - Drop-off minimized
4. Trust - Credibility established
5. Validation - Social proof integrated]]

## 1. READER JOURNEY DESIGN

[[LLM: Verify reader journey includes:

1. All chapters mapped in journey
2. Emotional curve defined
3. Touchpoints identified
4. Commitment progression designed
5. Risk points mapped]]

- [ ] Complete journey mapped for all chapters
- [ ] Emotional curve defined per chapter
- [ ] Engagement touchpoints identified
- [ ] Commitment curve designed
- [ ] Risk points mapped with interventions
- [ ] Valley management planned

## 2. CONTINUITY HOOKS

[[LLM: Verify continuity hooks:

1. Hook at end of each chapter
2. Variety of hook types used
3. Curiosity arc built
4. Transitions smooth
5. Natural, not forced]]

- [ ] Hook at end of each chapter
- [ ] Variety of hook types (curiosity, promise, story)
- [ ] Curiosity arc built through book
- [ ] Transitions feel natural
- [ ] Opening hooks at chapter starts

## 3. DROP-OFF MINIMIZATION

[[LLM: Verify drop-off prevention:

1. Risk chapters identified
2. Interventions planned
3. Checkpoints inserted
4. Progress reminders included
5. Pace varied appropriately]]

- [ ] High-risk chapters identified
- [ ] Intervention strategies defined
- [ ] Checkpoints of progress inserted
- [ ] Pacing optimized
- [ ] Quick wins in early chapters

## 4. AUTHOR CREDIBILITY

[[LLM: Verify author credibility:

1. Bio strategic and relevant
2. Expertise demonstrated (not just stated)
3. Authority hooks integrated
4. Story of why written included
5. Humble but confident tone]]

- [ ] Author bio strategic (not just credentials list)
- [ ] Expertise shown through examples
- [ ] Authority hooks in chapters
- [ ] Story of motivation included
- [ ] Humble but confident tone

## 5. SOCIAL PROOF

[[LLM: Verify social proof elements:

1. Minimum 3-5 testimonials included
2. 2-3 case studies integrated
3. Numbers and statistics used
4. Strategic placement throughout
5. Endorsement plan defined]]

- [ ] 3-5 testimonials in book
- [ ] 2-3 case studies integrated
- [ ] Numbers/stats used strategically
- [ ] Social proof on cover/back
- [ ] Endorsement gathering plan

## 6. REVIEW GENERATION

[[LLM: Verify review strategy:

1. Call-to-action points in book
2. Email sequence planned
3. Social media strategy defined
4. Response plan ready
5. Tracking process in place]]

- [ ] CTA for reviews in book
- [ ] Email sequence designed
- [ ] Social media posts planned
- [ ] Response strategy ready
- [ ] Tracking process defined
