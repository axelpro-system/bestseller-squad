# 04b - Book Editing Quality Checklist

**Agent:** @book-editor
**Phase:** Editorial Review (after Chapter Builder, before Engagement Designer)
**Pass Criteria:** 16+/20 points

---

## Section 1: Technical Quality (__/8 points)

### Grammar & Syntax (3 pts)
- [ ] Zero grammar errors (subject-verb agreement, tense, syntax) — 1 pt
- [ ] Punctuation correct and consistent (commas, periods, em-dashes, semicolons) — 1 pt
- [ ] Spelling and typos eliminated — 1 pt

### Prose Quality (3 pts)
- [ ] No redundant phrases ("in order to", "at this point in time", "the fact that") — 1 pt
- [ ] Active voice used consistently (>80% of sentences) — 1 pt
- [ ] Unnecessary qualifiers removed ("very", "really", "quite", "somewhat") — 1 pt

### Sentence Craft (2 pts)
- [ ] Sentence variety present (short, medium, long) — 1 pt
- [ ] Tense consistency within each section — 1 pt

---

## Section 2: Structural Quality (__/7 points)

### Content Delivery (4 pts)
- [ ] Chapter delivers on its stated objective — 2 pts
- [ ] Logical flow from opening hook to conclusion — 2 pts

### Organization (3 pts)
- [ ] Transitions between sections are smooth — 1 pt
- [ ] Subheadings are clear, descriptive, and consistently formatted — 1 pt
- [ ] Pacing is appropriate (no sections too dense or too thin) — 1 pt

---

## Section 3: Consistency (__/5 points)

### Terminology (2 pts)
- [ ] Same term used for same concept throughout the chapter — 1 pt
- [ ] Technical terms match usage in previous chapters — 1 pt

### Voice & Tone (2 pts)
- [ ] Author's voice preserved (editor didn't overwrite style) — 1 pt
- [ ] Tone matches previous chapters and project guidelines — 1 pt

### Formatting (1 pt)
- [ ] Formatting conventions followed (subheading style, bullets, bold, callouts) — 1 pt

---

## Total Score: __/20

### Verdicts

| Score | Verdict | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 16-20 | **PASS** | Ready for Engagement Designer |
| 12-15 | **NEEDS REVISION** | Specific issues listed; re-edit targeted sections |
| 0-11 | **REQUIRES REWRITE** | Return to Chapter Builder with structural feedback |
