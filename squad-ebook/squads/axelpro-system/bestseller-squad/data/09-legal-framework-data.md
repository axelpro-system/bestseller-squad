# Legal Framework Data (Source of Truth)

## Analysis Metadata

- **Reference:** Resolução CNJ nº 615/2025
- **Subject:** Governança e uso de Inteligência Artificial no Poder Judiciário
- **Deadline for Compliance:** Primeiro semestre de 2026
- **Status:** Mandatory (Judicial Expert compliance required)

---

## Core Principles from Resolution 615/2025

### 1. Transparência e Explicabilidade (Art. 3º)
- **Concept:** Experts must state when and how IA was used in the production of proof.
- **Application in Book:** Claude's role as a "drafting assistant" vs. "decision maker".
- **Safety Protocol:** Mandatory disclosure template in the "Laudo" Appendix.

### 2. Supervisão Humana (Human-in-the-Loop)
- **Concept:** No judicial report can be generated entirely by IA without human validation.
- **Application in Book:** The "Método Axelpro" emphasizes individual cross-verification of every AI-generated claim.

### 3. Privacidade e Proteção de Dados (LGPD)
- **Concept:** Handling of sensitive judicial data within AI environments.
- **Application in Book:** Guidelines on using Claude for Personal/Sensitive data (Data Privacy settings).

## Key Judicial Tools (CNJ)

### Berna (CNJ Tool)
- **Function:** Legal analysis assistant used by judges.
- **Strategic Insight:** Experts need to know how Berna works to ensure their reports are "Berna-friendly" (structured for AI consumption).

### Victor (STF)
- **Function:** Classification of extraordinary appeals.
- **Note:** Foundation of how the Judiciary views AI efficiency.

## CPC (Código de Processo Civil) Context

### Art. 473 (Conteúdo do Laudo)
- Experts must explain the method used. Resolution 615 updates the "modern method" to include AI-assisted analysis as long as documented.

### Art. 156-158 (Deveres do Perito)
- Maintaining technical authority while utilizing productivity tools.
